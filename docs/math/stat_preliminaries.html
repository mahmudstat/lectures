Data

Data: Information expressed in numbers (usually)

10, 23, 6, 16, 19, 25, 14, 12, 22, 28, 4, 21, 2, 7, 17

Types of Data

  1. Primary Data: Collected directly
  2. Secondary Data: Fetched from someone else
Examples
  1. A researcher collects rainfall data everyday for predicting future rainfall.
  2. An investigator collects family and geographical information of criminals.
  3. WHO tracks information of Covid-19 patients.
  4. An independent researcher uses information collected by WHO.
  5. A teachers borrowes marks graded by another teacher.
